Weekly Schedule

Where to get your class schedule: Log in to the MSU-IIT portal at myiit.msuiit.edu.ph . Then go to Academics > Class Schedule. Review your assigned subjects, days, and times, then manually encode them below by placing blocks under the corresponding day columns. Add your consultation hours manually. Include any administrative or research load as additional blocks under the Others category.

Click and drag on a day column to create a time block. Click a block to set its category and label. Drag the bottom edge to resize. Your schedule is saved automatically to this browser.
