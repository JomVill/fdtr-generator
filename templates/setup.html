Where to get your class schedule: Log in to the MSU-IIT portal at myiit.msuiit.edu.ph . Then go to Academics > Class Schedule. Include any administrative or research load as additional blocks under the Others category.

Click and drag on a day column to create a time block. Click a block to set its category and label. Drag the bottom edge to resize. Your schedule is saved automatically to this browser.
